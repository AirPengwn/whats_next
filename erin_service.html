About AmeriCorps service
Why AmeriCorps is a strong option for Erin

Erin has already completed one term of AmeriCorps NCCC, which means she understands how the program works, has demonstrated commitment to national service, and has the living-stipend lifestyle experience that trips up first-timers. A second AmeriCorps term in an environmentally focused Individual Placement (IP) is one of the most direct paths to building field credentials and making connections with federal and state agency professionals before graduate school.

Individual Placement (IP) positions are different from crew-based corps. An IP member is placed directly with a single federal agency, nonprofit, or land management office — working one-on-one alongside biologists, scientists, and managers. These are the positions most directly relevant to Erin's skills and most likely to lead to a graduate school connection or federal hiring pathway (Public Lands Corps Hiring Authority).

Education Award: Upon completing an AmeriCorps term, members receive a Segal Education Award that can be applied to tuition or qualified student loans. Full-year awards are currently ~$7,395; 1,200-hour terms ~$3,697; shorter terms proportionally less.

Environmental fellowships & bridge programs
EPA Greater Research Opportunities (GRO) Fellowship
Covers tuition + stipend for students in environmental careers. Competitive but strong profile match. Applications typically open in fall for the following academic year. Check epa.gov/careers for current cycle. Includes a paid summer internship at an EPA facility.
🔍 EPA GRO Fellowship
USDA Forest Service Pathways Recent Graduate Program
Federal hiring pathway for students who graduated within the past 2 years. Converts to permanent federal employment after one year. Biological science, hydrology, and GIS positions available. Posted on USAJobs. Search "Pathways Recent Graduate" + "environmental science" or "biological science."
