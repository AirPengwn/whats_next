Overview

Erin's BS in Environmental Science with hands-on field skills places her in a strong position for entry-level roles across federal agencies, state agencies, environmental consulting, and conservation nonprofits. Why the Wilmington District is the best fit

The USACE Wilmington District has jurisdiction over NC and SC — the exact geography where Erin studied wetlands and conducted her field research. Regulatory staff review Section 404 Clean Water Act wetland permit applications from developers, evaluate wetland delineation reports, and make jurisdictional determinations. Erin's GEOL 455 coursework used the Army Corps Delineation Manual directly — she would come in with working knowledge of the methodology these staff use every day.

Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plans are required for construction projects under the Clean Water Act. Erin's water quality regulatory knowledge (ENVH 310) and contamination assessment skills (GEOL 423) are relevant background.
