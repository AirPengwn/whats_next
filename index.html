What Makes Erin Competitive

The case for funded admission

Strong GPA (3.731) plus the rare combination of real field skills (wetland delineation, mammal & bird trapping, camera-trap tagging) and applied analytical work (GIS suitability analysis, the Hurricane Helene lead-contamination project). That mix is exactly what professors recruiting for funded RAs are looking for — technical chops they don’t have to teach from scratch.

Application strategy

Most funded spots are filled through direct contact, not application portals. The recommended path: identify 3–5 faculty per target school whose research aligns with wetlands, water quality, mammalogy, or environmental justice; email each one before the deadline; and lead the statement of purpose with the Hurricane Helene project — it’s the standout story.
